  <h1>What is Class 1 Digital and Power Repair?</h1>
  <br />
  <b>Class 1 Hearing Aid Digital and Power Repair includes the repair or
replacement of any of these electronic components while maintaining the
"original" hearing aid settings:</b>

  <ul>
    <li>transducer: microphones which receive sound waves</li>
    <li>speaker: a receiver which processes sound</li>
    <li>capacitors</li>
    <li>resistors</li>
    <li>amplifier</li>
    <li>copper wiring</li>
  </ul>
<br />
  <p>Warranty is provided for all components and labor, regardless of whether they were
functioning according to specification at the time of the initial repair
and were not the cause for the original failure.</p>
